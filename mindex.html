Navigating

Use the buttons above to navigate. If you click on a button, the page will open. Simple. Every main page has a back button but some pages don't. If a page doesn't have a back button, you can use the back button on your phone's navigation bar or your phone's back gesture. The back button on the top left corner is used to go back a whole main page. This means that it will jump back to the home page most of the time.
